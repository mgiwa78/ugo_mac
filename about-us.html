Who are we ?

UgoMac cleaning service was establish in 2020. We provide full services on all commercial janitorial and building maintenance solutions. Our aim is to change the status quo and introduce a benchmark for service levels in the line of cleanings.

Here at UgoMac, we have the experience required for domestic cleaning, so if you want to make sure your home is ready for inspection, call in the experts. So certain are we of the professional service we provide, we even guarantee it.

And, if you are not completely satisfied, all you have to do is let us know, within two working days, and we will return to clean the specific area free of charge. Before we carry out the cleaning, a UgoMac representative will visit the property to discuss which areas need cleaning.
Then a written estimate is provided, confirming the schedule and end of tenancy price.
